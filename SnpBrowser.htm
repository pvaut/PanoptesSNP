Welcome to this MalariaGEN community project, made up of many partner studies.

Each partner study comprises a group of researchers who are collaborating to address a specific scientific problem. Every study is different but what they all have in common is that they are carrying out genome sequencing on parasite samples collected in malaria-endemic areas and the population genetic data are being shared through this community project. The collaborators in a partner study nominate at least one contact person to represent them, who can answer any questions you may have about the study.
